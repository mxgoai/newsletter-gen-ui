About KnowsLetter

AI newsletter generator for B2B, healthcare, fintech, and marketing teams.

KnowsLetter is AI newsletter software that helps B2B, healthcare, and fintech teams create professional email newsletters in minutes. Use AI to generate content, choose sources, and automate marketing newsletter delivery.

  • • Smart prompts for structured, on-brand email newsletters
  • • Marketing newsletter automation with recurring schedules
  • • B2B-ready sections, bullets, and clear CTAs
  • • Reliable updates for healthcare and fintech audiences

FAQ

What does KnowsLetter do?
It creates AI-powered email newsletters from your prompts and sources, then schedules delivery.
Is it built for B2B and industry teams?
Yes. It supports B2B newsletter workflows and regulated industries like healthcare and fintech.
Can I schedule recurring newsletters?
Yes. You can set weekly recurring schedules and pick the delivery time.
Do I control the format and sections?
Yes. You define structure, sections, and formatting in your prompt.
Who is it for?
Teams that need fast, consistent newsletters: marketing, healthcare, fintech, and more.
