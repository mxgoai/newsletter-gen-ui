About KnowsLetter

Built for curious minds who want focused, intentional learning.

We built KnowsLetter for the curious minds who want to find information that truly matches their interests. It is an AI-powered newsletter curation platform for anyone who wants to learn with more focus and intention. Using KnowsLetter is effortless. There's no learning curve and nothing to set up. A simple prompt is all it needs.

KnowsLetter does thorough research of the topics you are interested in and brings you a well-structured digest straight to your inbox. Whenever you want to read it, on your schedule. It adapts to your context and works across languages. Whether you want a quick overview or a deeper exploration, it meets you where you are. Whatever you want to learn or make sense of, KnowsLetter can be your trusted partner in your learning journey.

  • • No setup or learning curve - just a prompt
  • • Thorough research and a structured digest
  • • Delivery on your schedule, when you want to read
  • • Adapts to language, context, and depth
FAQ expand_more
What is KnowsLetter? add remove
KnowsLetter is an AI-powered newsletter curation platform that helps you receive well-researched, personalized knowledge on topics you care about. Delivered directly to your inbox, on your schedule.
How is KnowsLetter different from regular newsletters? add remove
Traditional newsletters are written by someone else and sent to many people. KnowsLetter works the other way around. You tell it what you want to learn and it creates a newsletter specifically for you, based on your interests, requirements, and preferred depth.
How do I create a newsletter? add remove
Simply type your request in the prompt field describing what you want to learn. This can include the topic, format, sources, reading time, or anything else that matters to you.
Can I schedule newsletters for later? add remove
Yes. You can have the newsletter delivered whenever you want - immediately or schedule it for a future time or set them up as recurring digests. This allows you to learn consistently, without having to repeat the same request.
Does KnowsLetter support multiple languages? add remove
Yes. KnowsLetter supports multiple languages and adapts based on how you ask and what you need.
How much does it cost? add remove
KnowsLetter is completely free to use.
How does KnowsLetter handle privacy? add remove
Privacy is a core principle of KnowsLetter. It is open source and does not store your personal information or content. You can review how data is handled in detail on the documentation page.
